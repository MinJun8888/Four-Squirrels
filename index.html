Business Description

Business description

Four Squirrels is a business with interests in selling snack foods available mainly in Malaysia. It gathers a variety of different snacks to meet the needs of customers. It provides snack that is enjoyed by all generations in the region.


Four squirrels aim to provide a variety of unique and innovative snacks for customers, and customers can choose their favourite snacks according to their preferences. It offers snacks such as mamee monster snek mi (bbq flavor), mamee monster snek mi (ayam flavor), snek ku mimi, snek ku shoyuemi, Dahfa dried fish fillet, Tohato chocobi, Mister potato chips with different flavours, Twisties, Popo muruku ikan, and super ring. These snacks come in small packages that are easy for customers to carry easily.


Four Squirrels is a good quality brand and it can win the heart of the public range from kids and adults to old folks.
